About Us

The CarHunt Group has been established in the motor industry as of 2010. Since its up-start, it has been generating alot of traction and attention from alot of people around Ireland and even people abroad.

Founded by three entrepeneurs who go by the names Nabil Omar, Gareth Shaw and Vedran Badrov. They had a vision of making CarHunt one of the greatest car dealerships in Ireland.

CarHunt has been supplying New and Used cars to our valued customers for a decade now. Our clients know that they can rely on us to provide the best quality, affordable cars, where New or Used, and at the highest standard of customer service.

Not only do we supply casual New and Used cars, we also provide luxury cars from Lamborgini's, Range Rovers, Bentley's and much more. This is what puts CarHunt ontop of its competitors. Whether you want to rent a luxury car for a wedding or a special event, we have the affordable prizes for you.

In their succession of growing the company exponentially from a one to a 20 dealership, they still managed to preserve their values which is to always put their customers first and provide the best customer service possible. We have established over 30,000 great relationships with customers to date.
